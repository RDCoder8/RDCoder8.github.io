About Me

My name is Robert and I’m a software engineer and game developer. Over two years I’ve developed skills in web development and web design, using Javascript, HTML, CSS, and React. Along the way I’ve also gained skills in business analytics and financial forecast modeling with SQL, Tableau, and Excel. I’m certificated in both fundamental web development and data analytics from Udacity; an industry-focus training program. In addition to that I’m a graduate of Per Scholas’ Software Engineering Program which is sponsored by Google and Comcast. I’ve loved learning my entire life especially in the field of IT. Now I want to use these talents I’ve cultivated to build web applications, games, and tell stories through data.
